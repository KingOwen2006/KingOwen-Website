Week 1

In my first week of college I made a rubber duck during my first session of the year. This was a good ease into what would be coming up in the future — to help others refamiliarise themselves with Blender or for those that have never touched it. I already knew some methods since I’ve used Blender numerous times in the past.

Method used

  • Add a cube
  • Add a subdivision modifier (adds more geometry and makes it smoother)

Main repeated

  • Extruding (extending vertices into new ones)
  • Scaling
  • Rotating
  • Loop cuts (adds more geometry to manipulate vertices)

Overall, I’m happy with the look of the duck. I experimented with new keybinds that felt easier but needed more practice. The duck could use a more natural shape and smoother edges since it looks sharp. Next time I’ll use a reference instead of freehanding. Still, I like the general form, even if it’s a bit rough.
